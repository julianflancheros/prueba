Project Description

Tejidos C&S is a family business that did not have a digital presence and it was greatly affected by the COVID-19 pandemic, for this I offered them my knowledge about marketing digital for build its virtual brand facilitating communication between customers and the company.

When I finished the design process I continued with the content generation process to strengthen the brand, during this period I created material for both organic publications and paid publications. After a few months I gave them back their accounts so they could continue with the process strengthening the brand.

This process helped me to consolidate my knowledge in social media marketing and I am very happy with the result obtained and the help provided.
